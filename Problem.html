What is NeurPeace?

NeurPeace is an online program of psychological support, mental health, and self-discovery. We are a team ready to help you in difficult life situations and support you on the way to mental health and development.

Problem

  • Fear and distrust of psychology and doubts that it helps.
  • Fear of receiving psychological support without remaining private.
  • Sessions with psychologists are seen as shameful and a sign of weakness or shortcomings.
  • Ineffective Communication with psychological specialists.

Solution

  • The professionalism of specialists is detailed by information about psychologists and psychotherapists.
  • Broadcast of successful cases of other clients where they share how they solved their problems and how therapy helped them.
  • An opportunity to receive psychological support for those who want to keep their problems and conversations confidential.
